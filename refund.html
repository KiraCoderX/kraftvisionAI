Refund Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

At KRAFT VISION AI, we are committed to delivering high-quality AI-powered creative solutions and ensuring customer satisfaction. This Refund Policy explains when refunds may be issued for our products and services.

Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds may be issued under the following circumstances:

  • We are unable to deliver the agreed service or project within the committed timeline.
  • A technical issue on our side prevents us from delivering the requested service and the issue cannot be resolved within a reasonable time.
  • A payment was made in error or duplicated.

All refund requests are reviewed individually to ensure a fair resolution.

Refund Process

To request a refund, please contact us at:

contact@kraftvisionai.com

When submitting your request, please include:

  • Your name
  • The email address associated with your order
  • A brief description of the issue
  • Any relevant order or project details

Approved refunds are typically processed within 3–7 business days. Depending on your payment provider, it may take additional time for the refunded amount to appear in your account.

Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will generally not be issued in the following situations:

  • The requested work or service has already been successfully completed and delivered.
  • The customer changes project requirements after work has commenced.
  • Delays caused by the customer's failure to provide required assets, approvals, or information.
  • Services that have been completed according to the agreed scope.
  • Violations of our Terms of Service.

Project Delivery

For custom AI solutions, product photography, image generation, video generation, and other creative services, delivery timelines are communicated before work begins.

If KRAFT VISION AI is unable to deliver the agreed work within the committed timeline due to reasons under our control, the customer may be eligible for a full or partial refund, depending on the stage of the project.
