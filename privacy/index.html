Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how KRAFT VISION AI ("KRAFT VISION AI," "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use our services, we may collect:

  • Email address (provided during account registration or login)
  • Basic profile information, including your profile picture (if you sign in using Google or another supported authentication provider)
  • Images, videos, text, or other content that you voluntarily upload for AI processing
  • Usage information, analytics data, and technical diagnostics to improve our services
  • Device information, browser type, operating system, and IP address collected automatically for security and performance purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our AI-powered services
  • Personalize your account experience
  • Process requests and generate AI-created content
  • Improve platform performance and user experience
  • Monitor service reliability and security
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Respond to customer support requests

3. Cookies and Analytics

KRAFT VISION AI uses cookies and similar technologies to improve website functionality and understand how visitors use our platform.

These technologies may be used for:

  • Website analytics
  • Performance monitoring
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Improving user experience

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that some features of our website may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

4. Sharing Your Information

We only share information when necessary to operate and improve our services.

This may include trusted third-party providers that assist with:

  • Secure authentication
  • Payment processing
  • Website hosting and infrastructure
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • AI-powered content generation
  • Security and content delivery

These service providers only receive the information necessary to perform their services and are contractually obligated to protect your data.

We never sell your personal information.

5. Uploaded Content and AI Processing

Content you upload-including images, videos, text, and other media-is processed solely for the purpose of providing the requested AI-generated results.

KRAFT VISION AI does not permanently store your uploaded content or generated outputs unless specifically requested by the client.

For enterprise or custom AI model projects, data may be securely retained only with the client's explicit authorization for the purpose of training or fine-tuning custom AI models based on the client's business requirements.

Unless such authorization is provided, uploaded content is processed only to fulfill your request and is not retained or used for AI training.

We do not use customer content to train our AI systems without prior written consent.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal account information only for as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal obligations.

Uploaded files and generated content are not retained after processing unless:

  • You request that we store them.
  • Your organization requires storage for future access.
  • Storage is necessary for a custom AI training project that you have explicitly approved.

You may request deletion of your account and associated personal information at any time.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security practices designed to protect your information, including:

  • Secure HTTPS encryption
  • Access controls
  • Secure cloud infrastructure
  • Continuous monitoring for unauthorized access
  • Industry best practices for data protection

Although we take reasonable precautions, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable privacy laws, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Withdraw previously granted consent where applicable

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact@kraftvisionai.com.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 18 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will promptly delete it.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, legal requirements, or business practices.

When we make updates, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Your continued use of KRAFT VISION AI after any changes become effective constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
